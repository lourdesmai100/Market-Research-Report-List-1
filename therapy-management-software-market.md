<p><p>Therapy Management Software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a clinic's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing easier access, scalability, and lower initial costs, as users pay on a subscription basis. Both types aim to streamline therapy practices by managing patient records, scheduling, billing, and care coordination efficiently.</p></p>
