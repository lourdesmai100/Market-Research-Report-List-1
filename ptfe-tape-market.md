<p><p>PTFE tape, widely used in plumbing and sealing applications, is categorized by density into four types: Low Density, Standard Density, Medium Density, and High Density. Low Density tape is flexible and easy to apply, ideal for general sealing tasks. Standard Density offers a balance of flexibility and durability. Medium Density provides enhanced strength and sealing properties, while High Density tape is the most robust, suitable for high-pressure and extreme temperature applications. Each type serves specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
