<p><strong>The Industrial Steam Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire Tube Or Shell Boilers</li><li>Water Tube Boilers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial steam boilers are categorized into three main types: fire tube, water tube, and others. Fire tube boilers feature a series of tubes housing hot gases, heating water in the surrounding shell, making them efficient for low-pressure applications. Water tube boilers, on the other hand, circulate water through tubes heated by combustion gases, suitable for high-pressure requirements and offering faster steam production. Other types may include electric and biomass boilers, catering to specific industrial needs and efficiency standards.</p></p>
